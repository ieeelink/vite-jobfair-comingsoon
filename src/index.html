What is IEEE Job Fair?

One of the most prestigious events of IEEE Kerala Section, this program is determined to serve the goal of creating opportunities for engineering students and graduates.

The ultimate platform for companies to choose their successors from the largest talent pool in Kerala, it is aimed at refining the finest of the lot. This program broadens opportunities for undergrad students, graduates, and professionals alike to take a giant leap towards their lucrative career.
